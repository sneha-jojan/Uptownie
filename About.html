Our Story: Of Perseverance and Growth

Uptownie is a homegrown womenswear brand with its roots in pune, India. We empower Indian women to dress comfortably, fashionably, and authentically.

Our Story

Since the birth of Uptownie in 2015, we have connected with over 10 lakh customers. Today, our products have a nationwide presence and can be found on our website as well as e-commerce stores such as Myntra, Jabong, Flipkart, Amazon, Nykaa Fashion, and Ajio.

Our Process: Of Simplicity and Sustainability

We adopt global micro trends and design apparel, accessories, and footwear that align with the preferences of Indian women, considering their height, weight, complexion, figure, and body type. Each piece upholds the promise of quality, comfort, and durability. Our factory in Ganganagar, West Bengal, ensures high-quality production certified by SGS.

Our Founder: Of Leadership and Strength

Priyanka Agarwal, a Duke University graduate in Economics and Math, started Uptownie to empower Indian women with affordable, fuss-free clothing. With over 8 years of fashion experience and 10,000 unique designs, she crafts clothing that celebrates individuality and confidence.
